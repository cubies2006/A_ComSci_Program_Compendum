Checklists

Checklists are a quick and effective way of evaluating the usability of mobile games. They are intended to be printed out, and used as support for reviewing the application. Also Top ten usability guidelines can be effectively used as checklists when conducting a usability review.

At the time of writing, the following checklists are available:

    • User experience checklist for Symbian C++ applications

    • User experience checklist for Java™ ME applications

    • Terminology and localization checklist

 

The latest versions of the checklists can be downloaded from www.forum.nokia.com/usability
