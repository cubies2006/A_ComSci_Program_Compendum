Name: Jonathan Storm

ID: Public

Relatives: Franklin, Father (Deceased)...Mary, Mother (Deceased)...Susan, Sister (Invisible Woman of the Fantastic Four)...Reed Richards, Brother-in-law (Mister Fantastic of the Fantastic Four)...Franklin Richards, Nephew...Lyja, Estranged Wife who is an alien shape-shifting Skrull

Affiliates: Member of the Fantastic Four...Ally of Spider-Man.

Major Enemies:  Doctor Doom, Trapster, Wizard, Beetle, Plant Man, and all Fantastic Four Villains.

Powers: Mental ability to control ambient heat energy and envelop his body or portions of it in a fiery plasma that ranges from 780° Fahrenheit to his Super Nova Burst flame of 1,000,000° Fahrenheit...He also possesses the power of flight & Uses sculpted flames as weapons .

Origin: Johnny gained his powers from being bombarded with Cosmic Rays in a space flight on a Rocket Ship that wasn't properly shielded...Along with his Sister and two friends, The Fantastic Four became Marvel's first Super-Heroes in the Silver Age!
