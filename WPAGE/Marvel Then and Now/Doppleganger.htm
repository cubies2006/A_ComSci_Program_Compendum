Name: Nameless

ID: Not Applicable

Relatives: None Known.

Affiliates: Created in the Infinity War by The Magus, Former ally to Demogoblin, Carnage & Shriek.

Enemies: Spider-Man, Ghost Rider, Johnny Blaze, Venom

Powers: Doppleganger is basically an evil counterpart to Spider-Man and his powers mimic that of Spider-Man in speed, stamina, and strength...Plus has the extra agility and use of four extra arms...He cannot talk and is basically mindless.

Origin: Doppleganger was created along with many other Dopplegangers of heroes in the Marvel Universe such as Wolverine, Iron Man, and others...They were created by the Magus, a great foe of Warlock, who was a great participant in the Infinity War and represent the evil side of earth's heroes...They were all destroyed with the Magus' defeat...Except one!.

 

 

1st App.: Infinity War #1 

June 1992

Death: Spider-Man #37 

Origin:  Infinity War #1 

Spider-Man Appearances: Spider-Man #24 & 35-37...Web Of Spider-Man #93-96, 98 & 101-103... Amazing Spider-Man #378-380...Spectacular Spider-Man #201 & 202...Spider-Man Unlimited #1

Other Appearances: Spirits Of Vengeance #5...Marvel Comics Presents #112

 

 

After the Dopplegangers were destroyed in the Infinity War limited Series, Spider-Man rudely learned that not ALL the Doppleganger's were destroyed!...The Spider-Man Doppleganger with help from the Demogoblin survived and went on to cause a lot of havoc for Spider-Man.

 

 

 

Not only was the Spider-Man Doppleganger much trouble to his good counterpart, but the "Evil Twin" was a thorn in the side to Ghost Rider, Moonknight, and Johnny Blaze (The original Ghost Rider)...This story played out in Web Of Spider-Man #93-96 as well as Spirits Of Vengeance #5. He was later "killed" by Carnage while trying to save Shriek in the Maximum Carnage storyline.

 

Spider-Man Fear Rating:    3 (out of 10)
