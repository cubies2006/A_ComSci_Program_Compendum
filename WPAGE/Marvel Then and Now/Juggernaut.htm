Origin: Cain Marko has possession of a Ruby from the lost temple of Cyttorak...It gave him great power & turned him into a human Juggernaut with its transference of its mystical energies...His helmet and skullcap are comprised of an unknown indestructible metal.

The Juggernaut has been mainly a MAJOR foe of the X-Men since his introduction into the Marvel universe having close ties to their leader Professor X (his step-brother)...Juggy has fought Spider-Man a number of times including a memorable 2 part story in Amazing Spider-Man #229 & 230...It wasnt the last time these two would face each other though!

 

 

He began branching out with his indestructible force clashing against other's such as The Hulk, Thor, and even Dr. Strange....But as in the beginning of his career, Cain has most his problems with the X-Men...Lately, The Juggernaut has discovered that there are 7 others with powers similar to his and again has Spider-Man and an assortment of others to try and prevent them from wiping out the human race in The Eighth Day.
