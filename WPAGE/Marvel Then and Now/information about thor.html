Information About

Name: I - Donald Blake
II - Sigurd Jurlson
III - Jake Olson

ID: All Secret

Relatives:  Son of Odin & Gaea...Half-Brother of Loki (God of Mischief)...Half-Brother of Vidar

Affiliates: Co-Founding Member of The Avengers...Allies of Balder, Sif, Hercules, Captain America, Ironman, Warriors Three (Fandral, Volstagg, & Hogun).

Major Enemies: Loki, The Destroyer, Absorbing Man, Hela, Enchantress, Executioner, Mephisto, Surtur, Ymir, Midgard Serpent, Mangog, Grey Gargoyle, Ulik, Mister Hyde, Cobra.

Powers: Norse God of Thunder, Thor is one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe & his home-world of Asgard....Able to lift 100 Tons and more!...Immune to diseases & a relative immortal...His great weapon, an Uru Hammer named Mjolnir, is legend in itself...No one can lift it from the ground unless they are worthy...It returns from where it was thrown like a boomerang to its rightful owner & can open portals  to other dimensions...It can also summon rain, wind, thunder, & lightening.

Origin: Thor is a Norse God who really has no Origin but the 1st Thor, Don Blake gained his Alter Ego by stumbling upon a cave, trying to escape aliens and fighting them off with but a stick!...Little did anyone know was that the stick, when hit against the ground, turned into Mjolnir, Thor's great hammer!...After the ensuing battle, the aliens from Mars took a very long time to return to Earth.
