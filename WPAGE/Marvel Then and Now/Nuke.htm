Nuke

Agent Simpson was part success and part failure. The government wanted to create a new super soldier. They were only moderately successful. Nuke was certainly a powerful and devoted soldier, however he was also quite insane. Nuke was augmented and he also liked to supplement his strength with red, white, and blue pills that boosted his adrenaline. The madness was also an advantage because Nuke generally didn't question his sometimes somewhat illegal orders.

Daredevil #232- Nuke asks for a red pill on his way to his next mission. The Colonel flying the helicopter he rides in tries to explain, probably not for the first time, that he isn't in Vietnam looking for MIA's, but rather in Nicaragua. Nuke resets the kill counter on "Betsy" from 162 to 000, and promises he will break that number for the Colonel. Nuke says that they are holding "our boys" as he pulls on his gas mask. The red pill starts to work as Nuke leaps from the helicopter. He starts by firing napalm at the tower. Betsy's kill counter reads 005. The red is working full blast now as the adrenaline rushes in. Nuke fires, and Betsy reads 032. Nuke dodges bullets, and blows up a jeep. Betsy reads 045. Nukes says, "Our boys" as he continues to fire. Betsy reads 088 as Nuke goes totally red. Later, Nuke asks for a white, and asks where he is going now. The Colonel says that the General is sending him to New York. Betsy reads 197. Elsewhere the General contacts Kingpin and tells him that Nuke, Agent Simpson, is on his way, and that he awaits the goods Fisk promised him. On the plane over Nuke asks for a blue. The fight attendant comes to tell Nuke that the beer he asked for isn't made anymore. She offers him a substitute. Nuke grabs the beer and her wrist. He demands to know where the beer is brewed. She tells him Milwaukee. The Colonel tells him not to worry it's an American beer. Finally Nuke stands before Kingpin. Kingpin tells Nuke that his predecessor, the first super-soldier, had in much easier. That these are trying times when patriots are laughed at. Kingpin says Nuke reminds him of his son, also a veteran. Kingpin says that he is persecuted as a criminal. Kingpin says that legislation threatens free enterprise. He says that he and a few like minded allies have united against those unpatriotic ones that are trying to tear down. Kingpin says that one man threatens to destroy what they have built with his friends in the press. Nuke asks where he is. Kingpin sends him to Hell's Kitchen. The Colonel drops Nuke off low. He tells him to skip the Napalm because these are Americans. Daredevil hears from four blocks away a baby's lung collapsing from Nuke's gunfire. Nuke moves up on the helicopter. They're being shot at by another copter. Nuke destroys it. Just then a billy club bounces off Nuke's temple, and back to Daredevil. Nuke asks for a red.

Daredevil #233- A water tower explodes as Nuke shoots at Daredevil. When Daredevil is out of sight the Colonel tries to abort the mission, but Nuke leaps from the copter saying, "Won't let you down…won't let…our boys down". Daredevil hears people dying everytime Nuke fires his gun. He kicks nukes face, but Nuke is faster than he is and he hits Daredevil with the butt of his gun. The Colonel shoots at Daredevil from the copter. Daredevil tries for a nerve center, but it doesn't affect Nuke. Nuke brings the gun down on the back of Daredevil's head. Daredevil pokes Nuke's eyes, but he doesn't feel the pain. Again Daredevil is introduced to Nuke's gun. Daredevil tries to sever a muscle, but nearly breaks his fingers on Nuke's forearm. Daredevil punches the elbow, but is rewarded with a backhand in the face. Daredevil kicks him off the top of the building into some power lines. Nuke falls onto an on fire police car. Nuke fires up, but Daredevil leaps onto him and knocks him through the roof. Daredevil takes Nuke's gun and rolls off as the car explodes. Nuke emerges on fire with a knife. Daredevil breaks of the butt of the gun on Nuke's face. The Colonel starts to shoot, but Daredevil blows up the helicopter with Nuke's gun. The Avengers arrive. Daredevil has Nuke by the throat yelling that he wants to hear that it was Kingpin that sent him. Nuke just keeps asking for a white. The Avengers take Nuke into custody. As Nuke begs for a white Captain America looks dejectedly downward. The General is taking to the Kingpin as Captain America barges in. The General says Cap isn't cleared for information. Cap doesn't leave, but breaks into the sub-level six, the Vault. Cap finds out about Agent Simpson and the super soldier info. Nuke is shouting for a red, but can't have one right now because of how jumpy the reds make him. The General says that Nuke is being shipped off against the enemy. Nuke says that the enemy is here. He grabs the newspaper and says, "This is the enemy…this..they lie about us…make us feel ashamed". He grabs a guard's gun and shoots everyone in the room killing them all but the General. He breaks off his handcuffs, and reaches for the reds. Nuke takes the whole bottle and says, "I won't let you down, sir…They try…to make our boys ashamed". Cap goes up when he hears the siren. Nuke pushes some soldiers down taking a rifle. His last shot bounces off Cap's shield. Cap pushes him through the outside wall. They fall through the building next door. Cap struggles up, and picks up Nuke. When Cap comes through the roof the helicopter shoot at them. Daredevil pushes the over. Daredevil tries to move Nuke, but another helicopter shoots him up. Cap stops the other soldiers as Daredevil tries to drive Nuke to a hospital. Nuke doesn't want to go. Daredevil takes Nuke to the Daily Planet and puts Nuke's dead body on Ben Urich's desk. While the Kingpin isn't ever charged with anything it does cause him to be shunned by the legitimate business world.
