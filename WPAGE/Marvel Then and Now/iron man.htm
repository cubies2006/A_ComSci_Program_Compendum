Name: Tony Stark

ID: Secret

Relatives: Parents, Howard & Maria both deceased...Morgan (Cousin)...Close friends Harold "Happy" Hogan & Virginia "Pepper" Potts are very much like family.

Affiliates: Founding member of the Avengers, West Coast Avengers & Force Works.

Major Enemies: Mandarin, Titanium Man, Crimson Dynamo, Unicorn, AIM, the Controller, Firebrand, Melter, Whiplash/Blacklash, Dr. Doom.

Powers: Superhuman strength enhanced by armored exoskeleton along with many weapons including Repulsor Rays, Disruptor Beam, Pulse-Bolts, and Energy shields...Has supersonic flight speed...Originally wore armor to aid damages heart which is now repaired by surgery.

Origin: An almost casualty of the Vietnam war, Tony Stark was held captive by Communist leader Wong-Chu & was allowed to live if he invented a powerful weapon...And he did!...But it was used by Tony against Wong-Chu and a legend was born!
