Powers:  Although the Kingpin appears extremely obese, he is in Peak human condition with peak human strength & agility...He is skilled in the Martial Arts and Sumo Wrestling, and is an extraordinary hand to hand combatant...He is also a gifted organizer and planner with much knowledge in Political Science...Carries an "Obliterator Cane" walking stick that contains a laser beam that shoots 300 watts & also has a diamond stickpin that can fire sleeping gas.

Origin: The kingpin has no superhuman powers but he has made his way to the top of organized crime by shear strength and intellect gained after studying Political Science and practicing Sumo Wrestling and Martial Arts.
