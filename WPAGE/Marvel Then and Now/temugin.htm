Temugin

Temugin is the son
of the Mandarin.
He has precious few
memories of his father
and most of them
involve his father
taking him to the
monastary where he
was raised and trained.
Finally one day, Temugin
received a package
containing the hands
of the Mandarin.
The hands bore all the
rings of power and
Temugin knew that he
was honor bound to
fulfill his father's wishes
for him. Temugin has already
had measured success
against Iron Man defeating
him without the aid of any
of the rings of power
(Temugin thought that they
gave him an unfair advantage).
