Bullseye

Bullseye always had perfect aim. He was all signed up to become a major league baseball pitcher when he opted instead to volunteer for the war. The war brought out the killing side of Bullseye and the world is a poorer place for the transition. Bullseye is recognized as the top assassin in the business.

Daredevil #131- flashback- Bullseye is seen in Vietnam. He was a very gung ho soldier and wanted to destroy the Cong. Bullseye was out patrolling one day when he heard a guerilla come up behind him. Bullseye’s gun jams, but before his opponent can draw his own gun Bullseye takes his gun and throws it through the guerilla’s chest. Bullseye always had perfect aim, and before he enlisted for the war he was signed up for Major League Baseball.

Daredevil #131- flashback- After the war Bullseye became a mercenary in Africa.

Daredevil #131-Bullseye throws a paper plane through a closed window. Mr. Hunnicutt, the man inside the room, unfolds the plane to read, “Pay me $100,000 or I will kill you!!! –Bullseye” Mr. Hunnicutt says that someone is going to answer for this tasteless joke. Bullseye is suddenly beside him and says that he doesn’t think so. Hunnicutt asks who he is. Bullseye says that his real name would mean nothing, but that he will recognize his stage name- Bullseye. Bullseye asks if Hunnicutt has the money that he wants here. Hunnicutt says that he doesn’t have it here, but before Hunnicutt can explain Bullseye picks up the pen off the desk and throws it into Hunnicutt’s neck. Bullseye tells him not to take it personally. Later Daredevil comes by to see what is described as a gruesome murder. Hunnicutt is laid against the wall with a bullseye painted on him and the wall he leans on. “This is an example to all who refuse to pay Bullseye.” Jake Conover asks Daredevil to come by his office. He tells him some things that an “informant” told him about Bullseye’s past. Daredevil correctly guesses that the one who told Conover was Bullseye himself. Daredevil swings away only to find that a grenade has just flown right by him. The blast causes Daredevil to land. As he comes down he is greeted by Bullseye. Bullseye says that his reputation is well deserved. Daredevil charges Bullseye, but Bullseye kicks a few cans of trash at Daredevil. Bullseye says that he has no wish to finish him now however. Bullseye tells Daredevil to follow him. Bullseye walks him towards the police where a bunch of cameras are around. Bullseye says that he wanted an audience. Bullseye says that he has men ready to shoot into the crowd if the police try to stop him. Daredevil leaps at Bullseye. Bullseye throws a rope around Daredevil’s throat. Finally the lights come on and they are standing in a circus.
