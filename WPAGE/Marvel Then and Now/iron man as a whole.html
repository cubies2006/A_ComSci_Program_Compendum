As A Whole

     Iron Man was created by Marvel's leading creator Stan Lee (above) in 1963. Iron Man first appeared in Tales of Suspense # 39, and appeared in 7 other comics, including Force Works and the Avengers. In his own title, the first series was 332 issues, and had many great plots and armors. In 1996 the series was canceled to relaunch the series into a darker setting.

      Recently in1996, Iron Man appeared in Heros Reborn, Iron Man. This series existed out of the normal Marvel Universe and had a darker look on the Avenger. His origin was retold as the Hulk crushed Tony in the experimental Promethium armor, forcing Tony to don the armor to save his life.
Tony's attitude also changed towards his work and friends. He seemed shadier, and colder to the people he cared about.  After 13 issues the series was canceled.

      Currently Iron Man stars in Heros Return: Iron Man which started in 1997 and is still continuing. This series is back in the regular Marvel universe and mostly continues from volume one; While the whole second series is mostly forgotten about.

      But still, after over 36 years, Tony Stark and Iron Man still retain much the same over the years. He still fights personal demons and the whole book isn't focused on just his armor. Instead it focuses on his personality and feelings towards his life and friends.
