Ultron

Ultron is the robotic creation of Hank Pym even if he didn't remember doing it. Hank managed to create an artificial intelligence: Ultron-1. Ultron-1 then shot Hank with a hypnotic ray causing him to forget his newest invention. Ultron managed to escape and upgrade himself until finally he confronted the Avengers as Ultron-5. Ultron was also responsible for creating the Vision, who possesses the brain patterns of Simon Williams, Wonder Man, thus creating a very dysfunctional family out of Earth's Mightiest Heroes.
