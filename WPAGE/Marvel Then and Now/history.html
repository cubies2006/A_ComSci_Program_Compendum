There have been many great teams through the years on this great site. Many have come and gone, but like a dead comic character...like Colossus, are never forgotten. We present to you SHSD's...


10. The Hellfire Club
Although appearing to be inactive at times, the Hellfire Club was a pretty strong staple in SHSD for quite some time. They had many great RP's and very powerful members in the forms of Magneto, Polaris, and Phoenix. Their greatest achievement was taking over the United States and "kidnapping" the island of Manhattan.

9. Avengers
Marvel Comics' main superhero team. This team was started and is currently led by Iron Man. It has had many members and is still one of SHSD's strongest teams as of this writing. If Role Plays are picked up and regulated, they would definitely be well on their way to being one of the top 5 greatest teams ever.

8. The Brotherhood
One of SHSD's best villain teams to date. They have gained status as being the most deceitful and underhanded team in the site's history by bending some site rules (which wasn't allowed and won't be again). The team was originally led by the Blob, who later switched to lead as the Toad, who is now playing as Avalanche as of this writing. He is, quite possibly, the mastermind behind it all. The current team leader is Mystique. Their highest accolades are destroying the X-Mansion and the SHIELD Hellicarrier along with assassinating President Lex Luthor. They are mainly a mutant team with a few extra bad guys added in for good measure.

7. Z-Warriors
Back in the SHSD's Golden Age, when characters of all genres were part of the site, there existed the Z-Warriors. The Z-Warriors was the second team ever on SHSD and was also one of the best, fighting the X-Men in the first ever team battle. They lost, but still gave a solid effort. The team was comprised of characters from the hit cartoon, Dragonball Z. The team was led by Piccolo, the first SHSD recruit ever.

6. Wakandan Special Forces
WSF was the third team ever created in SHSD's history. It was led by both Black Panther and Deadpool and served as the Golden Age's comic relief team, this time not going OOC because most of the characters were played right. Through they're role plays, the WSF had a very interesting roster of NPC characters to go along with their crazy role plays, such as Freefall, Green Arrow, Savage Dragon, Conan the Barbarian, and Everett K. Ross. They defeated the X-Men in the second team battle on the site. This team later evolved into Justice For Hire which later evolved into...

5. S.H.I.E.L.D.
This team was definitely the most powerful at one point. Led by Nick Fury and Black Panther, they were alliers and sometimes enemies to The World's Strongest. SHIELD brought along all of its original NPC characters from the WSP/JFH and used them well as agents of SHIELD. Later on in SHIELD's existence, Magneto joined on as another co-leader.

4. Rough Justice
This team was SHSD's greatest non-mutant team at one point. With founding members like the Punisher, the Spot(yes, the Spot...the goofiest Spider-Man villain ever), Vash the Stampede, and Superman, Rough Justice had some of the site's greatest role players and most powerful characters at the time. They had an epic feud with the Four Horsemen of Apocalypse. Unfortunately, the team eventually fell to inactivity as many members computers failed on them and left only a few more members around, but without the whole original team, it fell through.

3. The Four Horsemen of Apocalypse
The most dominant and feared villain team on the site at one point. Since it was the ONLY villain team at that point, they had to fight off just about everyone, and they did a heck of a job succeeding! The Four Horsemen were led by Apocalypse and consisted of Death, Famine, War, and Pestilence. The Horsemen ranks were rotated frequently at soem points. Horsemen included Archangel, Bloodstorm, Juggernaut, Omega Red, Wolverine (original), Geese Howard, and Diablo. They had many memorable fights against Superman, Punisher, and various other Rough Justice members as well as the X-Men, then led by Gambit. Their most memorable fight, without a doubt, was a team battle between them and the X-Men in Magda Square. It was possibly the single greatest battle in the site's history. Sadly, though, the team fell apart when members left the site and the main leaders left after the main MOD took a leave from the site. The Horsemen were the greatest villain team until...

2. Weapon X
Though only a month old as of this writing, Weapon X has already proved themselves to be the greatest threat ever seen in the history of SHSD. This leaderless team not only takes names and kicks ass, but they might even assimilate you into their team if they so wish, such was the case with the first and greatest team in SHSD history, the X-Men. They had been slightly inactive for a while until Weapon X pounded on their doorstep, and in the most violent, and long battle preparation role play series ever, eventually defeated them in a massive set of weekend battles with a score of 4-3, Weapon X. During the role play saga, Weapon X gave readers a taste of what was to come by unexpectedly taking over the Hulk and bringing him into the team. As of this writing, the X-Men no longer exist and are now part of Weapon X. After taking over the X-Mansion and the X-Men themselves, Weapon X turned to the Brotherhood and through a foolproof role play, destroyed their secret Muir Island base and leveled Mutant Town for good measure. It was later revealed that the main funder of the Weapon X Program is the President of the United States himself, George Bush. Weapon X then defeated the Brotherhood in an epic battle. After taking apart every super team the SHSD had to offer, Weapon X collapsed as the X-Men escaped their powerful grasp, killing the Director in the process.

1. X-Men
The team goes down in history just for being the first team ever to be introduced into the world of SHSD. Not only are they the first, but as this ranking shows, they are the best. The X-Men were founded by Gambit (now Wolverine) and Wolverine (original) when there were only six members on the site. Eventually, the team grew stronger and stronger as more mutants joined. Among them was Nightcrawler, who currently plays as Bloodstorm, who has also led the team on a few occasions. In the beginning, the X-Men accepted every mutant that joined the site, even Deadpool for a short period of time before he formed his own team, the Wakandan Special Forces. The X-Men battled every worth-while team and character ever on the site, defeating just about every one of them. There have been so many X-Men over SHSD's years of existence that even SHSD's creator can't count, like Gambit, Wolverine, Cyclops, Storm, Bloodstorm, Mimic, Colossus, Nightcrawler, Morph, Domino, Jubilee, Phoenix, Beast, Archangel, Bishop, Deadpool, X-Man, Iceman, Cable, Rogue, Psylocke, and many, many more. Many characters have even been played by various players. The X-Men were once the largest team in SHSD's history. Not only have they been the largest, but until the formation of Weapon X, they were the most powerful the site had ever seen. The X-Men, as stated in Weapon X's entry, were defeated and assimilated into the Weapon X team, destorying the X-Men. It was not until months later that they finally returned after breaking free of Weapon X's control. The X-Men are forever scarred, however, as Professor Xavier himself gave up on the dream and left his X-Men.

Weapon X seemingly coming from nowhere and defeating the X-Men. 4. Biggest Flop...EVER
The Onslaught tournament. The idea was cool and excited everyone. The prize was a large amount of SD bucks, stat points, and a complete set of Origin comic books to the tournament winner. Since Origin was delayed, however, the MOD running it got bored of the entire saga because it was so long and just quit doing it and took a long hiatus from SHSD, giving Punisher the reigns to the site. Punisher later relinquished his MOD duties and gave them to Deadpool and Black Panther, who controlled the site for several months until the creator, Wolverine, finally came back, bringing in a gigantic flood of new players with him.

20. Biggest Comback in SHSD History
Batman vs. Sage in the Secret Wars. Sage, an opponent that is already stronger than Batman, draws in to fight Batman...who only has one hit point left! Somehow, Batman lays the smackdown on Sage, bringing her ALL the way down to 3 HP before she finally hits him. He hit her with two Nullification gun blasts, rolled his power twice, and landed two sleeping gas attacks! He lost the fight, but definitely showed courage, beating Sage to her last legs and causing her to lose to Shadowcat, a relative new character, in the next round.
