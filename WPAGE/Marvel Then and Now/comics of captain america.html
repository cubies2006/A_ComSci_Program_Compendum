Captain America #275-278- Zemo sits and looks at a picture of his family building anger towards Captain America. Zemo activates a monster from within S.H.E.I.L.D. headquarters. The monster frees Vermin and kidnaps Arnie Roth and his roomie Micheal as he goes. Baron Zemo II is teamed with Primus (disguised as Arnim Zola). Cap is taken to a castle by a genetically altered human. Primus morphs into Steve Rogers to go and wreck Cap's new life as Zemo percieves Cap did to his life. This was before Zemo's dreams of conquest. His plan was to imprison Cap and Vermin (who had outlived his usefulness to Zemo) in chains while projecting the minds of Arnie and Micheal into two genetic rampaging monsters. Cap and Vermin break free, however Vermin fights back and kills Micheal. Zemo is pleased that Cap's relationship with Arnie is ruined. Arnie forgives Cap saying he was only angry from frustration. Vermin flees. Arnie and Cap get to Zemo and Anrie(!) knocks Zemo out, when Zemo escapes we see that Vermin is aboard the ship and attacks Zemo

Captain America #357-362-Zemo contracts Bartoc's Brigade to steal the skeleton of Ulysses Bloodstone. Zemo then has his psychic detective (?!?) to help him locate the five bloodstones. Bartoc's Brigade defeats Diamondback and place her in the box with Bloodstone's skeleton. Cap later frees her. Then with Diamondback Cap is taken captive and placed on a "Wheel of Death", with Zemo and Bartoc's Brigade, by a tribe of Incas(?!?). Cap busts everybody out and Zemo and the Brigade escape. Zemo now has two of the five bloodstones. Bartoc scuba dives for the next bloodstone. Bartoc, Zaran, and Machete fight Cap until a group of sharks causes everyone to flee. Diamondback gets the drop on Baron Zemo, but Helmut is saved by his dwarf psychic. Bartoc saves Cap from the sharks. Diamondback floors the dwarf. When the trio make it back onto the boat they find Diamondback holding Zemo captive. Bartoc throws her the newly acquired stone, but when she reaches out for it Zaran tags her with a knife. After she falls overboard Zemo discovers Diamondback stole the two stones in his case. Cap saves Diamondback again. Zemo has the Brigade enter a pyramid to find the next bloodstone. A mummy gives Cap his bloodstone, and John Jameson, who is watching Zemo has the other two. Bartoc managed to grab the stone Cap dropped. That gives Zemo two pieces and Cap's team two pieces. The psychic warns Zemo that someone else is also after the stones. Bartoc's Brigade tracks the last stone to a Japenese restaurant where they interrupt a resurrection with the stone. They defeat all those who oppose them and Machete slices open the old man who swallowed the stone. When they leave Captain America is waiting for them. Bartoc arranges for Zemo and Cap to meet to discuss Cap's two pieces. When they meet Zemo threatens to blow up a city block in Tokyo if he doesn't get the pieces. Diamondback finds Heinrech Zemo's body while spying in Zemo's ship. Cap finds his pilot down and his gems gone. When he gets back to Zemo a flash of power comes out of Zemo's ship. Then the corpse of Heinrich Zemo is floating reanimated. Zemo notices that it doesn't seem like his father. The body uses the Hellfire Helix to activate the volcano. Crossbones stoe Cap's stones, but when they were near the other 3 they united on Zemo's head. Whoever is within Heinrich's body it isn't him. Crossbones manages to use a crossbow to break the stone off Zemo's head. Zemo's body falls down a hole, and Zemo jumps in after his father.
