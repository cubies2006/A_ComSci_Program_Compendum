    Information About

    Real Name: Frank Castle

    Height: 5'10''

    Weight: 195 lbs

    Eyes: Blue

    Hair: Black

    Hometown: New York City

         After a bloody tour of duty in Vietnam, Frank Castle came home to live a peaceful life with his family. But that came to a sudden, violent end when his wife and children were killed in a botched mob hit. Since then, Castle has taken up the guise of the Punisher, and dedicated himself to eliminating criminals-permanently! Viewed as a vigilante hero by some and criminal by others, the Punisher knows only one kind of justice- the kind that comes out of the barrel of a gun!
