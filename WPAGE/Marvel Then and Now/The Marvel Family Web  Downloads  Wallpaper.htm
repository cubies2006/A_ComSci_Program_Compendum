Installing Wallpaper - Win 95/98/NT/ME/2000

  1. Find out how big your screen is. Shrink your browser window and click with the right mouse button on an empty piece of desktop. A pop-up menu should appear. Select 'Properties'. A box will appear with tabs across the top. Select the 'Settings' tab and on the right-hand side of the box, it should say 'Desktop Area' and tell you how big your screen is.
  2. Go back to this webpage. Click on the PC link above that has the same resolution as your screen. For resolutions bigger than 1024x768 use the 1024x768 option. The picture should now load in your browser.
  3. Click on the picture with your right mouse button and a menu will appear. Select the 'Set As Wallpaper' option.

Installing Wallpaper - Mac OS

  1. Select and click on the image size to suit your resolution.
  2. Once the image is open click on the image and hold the mouse button down, select 'Save This Image As' from the menu and save it to your hard drive.
  3. From the 'Apple Menu' go to 'Control Panels' and select 'Desktop Pictures'.
  4. Click on the picture icon and click on 'Select Picture' and open your chosen image from the folder/desktop where it is saved.
  5. Click on 'Set Desktop'
