Profile of Mephisto

BIBLIOGRAPHY

First appearance: Silver Surfer Vol.1 #3
Origin issue: Doctor Strange Vol.3 #8, Silver Surfer Vol. 3 #45

BIOGRAPHICAL DATA

Real name: Unrevealed; “Mephisto” short for “Mephistopheles”, is a name given to him by human beings who believed him to be the legendary Satan.
Other current aliases: Satan, Mephistopheles, Lucifer, Beelzebub, the Devil
Former aliases: Nick Scratch, Legion, Hellstrom, Miles Gorney, Nick Cloot, Donald Blake
Current Occupation: Ruler of an extra dimensional land of the dead, sometime called “Hell” or “Hades”
Former occupation: None known
Citizenship: Hell
Legal Status: None on Earth
Place of birth: Unrevealed
Marital Status: Unrevealed, perhaps inapplicable
Known relatives: Victoria Wingate Hellstrom (wife, deceased), Daimon Hellstrom (Hellstorm, son), Patsy Walker Hellstrom (Hellcat, daughter-in-law), Satana Hellstrom (daughter, deceased), Mephista (daughter), Blackheart (son), Malevolence (daughter in analternate future) Known confidants: None
Known allies: Blackheart, Mpehista, Malevolence, (former) Flying Dutchman, the Six-Fingered Hand, Darkoth the Demon, Thanos, Loki, Commander Thule, Suicide, Mys-Tech Board
Major Enemies: the Silver Surfer, Thor I, Dr. Doom, John Blaze, Hellstorm, Hellcat, Satana (deceased), the Fantastic Four, Dracula, Topaz, the Defenders, Zarathos, Dr. Strange, Franklin Richards, the Beyonder, Master Pandemonium, the X-Men, the Avengers, Galactus, the Hulk, Glorian, the Shaper of Worlds, Baron Mordo, Satanish, Thog, Agatha Harkness, daredevil, Ghost Rider II, Odin, She-Hulk, Cloak & Dagger, Spider-Man, Thanos, Thor II (Thunderstrike), Adam Warlock, Dark Angel, Mys-Tech Board
Usual base of operations: Hell
Former base of operations: None
Current group membership: None
Former group membership: Six-Fingered Hand
Extent of education: Unrevealed


POWERS AND ABILITIES

Intelligence: Immeasurable
Strength: Unrevealed (can be magically augmented to potentially incalculable levels)
Speed: Enhanced human
Stamina: Immeasurable
Durability: Godlike
Agility: Enhanced human
Reflexes: Enhanced human
Fighting skills: Unrevealed; Mephisto usually engages in use of his magical powers in favor of physical combat
Special skills and abilities: Vast knowledge of magical lore
Superhuman physical powers: Aside from the above listed attributes: immortality, vast inherent supernatural power and virtually unlimited ability to manipulate magical energies for virtually any purpose
Superhuman mental powers: Unrevealed
Special limitations: Mephisto cannot subjugate the will of another being without the victims submission, it does not matter if the submission is dilberate or not.
Source of superhuman powers: Demonic heritage and manipulation of the forces of magic. Mephisto once claimed that he and other “elder” demons were created by the omnipotent entity who eventually became the Infinity Gems.
