H E R O

As difficult as it was for human Eric Masterson to live up to the responsibilities of a thunder god, he faced his greatest challenge when he battled the malevolent Mephisto for control of the Hammer of Thor- without the use of his superhuman powers! Now back in control of the Urœ Hammer, Eric strives once again to follow in the footsteps of the son of Odin, defending the Earth and protecting the innocent from the forces of evil- and proudly bearing the name of the Mighty Thor!
