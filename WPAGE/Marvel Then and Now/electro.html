Name: Maxwell Dillon

ID: Public

Relatives: Unknown

Affiliates: Fellow members of the Sinister Six, Frightful Four, & the Emissaries of Evil...Ally of the Wizard, Trapster, Blizzard, & the Purple Man.

Enemies: Spider-Man, Daredevil, Captain America, Fantastic Four, Falcon, X-Man, Shocker.

Powers: Ability to bodily generate electrostatic energy at 1,000 volts per minute and store up to 1 million volts. He can kill a normal powered man at distances of as few as 10 feet...he can override any electrical powered device and manipulate it mentally...He also has recently gone through a "Power Surge" in the pages of Amazing Spider-Man.

Origin: After being struck by lightning, an unusually configured magnetic field was created. Maxwell Dillon, a hire wire lineman, was right in the middle of it!...A mutagenic change occurred and soon followed a life of crime.
