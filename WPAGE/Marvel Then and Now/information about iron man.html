Information About

Real Name: Tony Stark

Height: 6'1''

Weight: 205 lbs

Eyes: Brown

Hair: Black

Hometown: New York City

     Despite his vast fortune, international business empire, and unparalleled technological genius, Tony Stark's destiny is a tragic one. Although it has made him one of Earth's mightiest heroes, the invincible iron armor of Iron Man is not only his finest creation, but also his prison. For Tony Stark is a victim of a deadly nerve disease - he must constantly wear his armor. Without it, Stark would die, and the hero known as Iron Man would be no more!

     Memorable Quote: "Super hero. Would the world really think that... if they knew the full story of the frail, mortal man who lives inside this shell?"- Iron Man #268 (May 1991)
