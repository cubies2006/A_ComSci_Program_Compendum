Una-Rogg

Una-Rogg is the daughter of the traitorous Yon-Rogg, and sister to the vengeful Zey-Rogg. Una-Rogg as a Kree women has the inherant ability to bend Kree men's wills. All women have the ability, but in order to keep the female of the race under their thumbs the men of the Kree have a sort of mental circumcision that cuts them off from this ability. This operation sadly has a side effect cutting the women off from their pleasure centers.

A tiny percentage of Kree females also develop the ability to drain men of their life force simply by kissing them. Because of this ability all women are subjected to the mutilation. Yon-Rogg saved his daughter and falsified the documents. Coincidentally, Una-Rogg is one of the rare females who develop the life force draining ability.
