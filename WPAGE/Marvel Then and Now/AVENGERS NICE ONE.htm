AVENGERS

T E A M

As ancient England slept soundly under the protection of the knights of the Round Table, so does the Earth rest safely today under the watchful eye of its mightiest champion- The Avengers! Through many years and many changes, The Avengers - Captain America, Thor, Vision, Hercules, Sersi, Crystal and the Black Knight- remain committed to defending life and liberty against the forces of evil both on this world and others, as evidenced by their participation in the recent interstellar conflict known as Operation Galactic Storm!
