Zey-Rogg

Zey-Rogg is the son of the traitorous Yon-Rogg. Yon-Rogg died fighting against Captain Mar-Vell. Today, Captain Mar-Vell has passed away as well, but Zey-Rogg has a new focus for his hatred... the son of Mar-Vell, Genis-Vell. Known by his father's namesake, Captain Marvel now has a thorne in his side.
