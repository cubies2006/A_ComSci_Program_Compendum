The Origin of the Silver Surfer Page 2


My will had never been subverted before my hunger led me to a world known as Zenn-La.   Its inhabitants were insignificant to me.  I required the planet's energies.  Whatd did I care if its people were destroyed in the process?

They were peaceful scientists who had long since fogotten how to defend themselves.  And so I prepared to feed.  One of their number dared approach and petition me to spare his world.  He was Norrin Radd.
