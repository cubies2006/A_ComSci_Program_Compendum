The Rhino

Not much is known about the grey giant before his career in super-villainy. Rhino has had several motivations since his change. First was simply as a work for hire muscle man, or to show his power. Then after being stuck within his suit for a bit Rhino decided that he wanted out. But escaping takes money, money to remove the Rhino suit that was bonded to him. Once he suceeded in getting out of his suit he realized the danger around him of being normal. So back in the suit. Rhino is now working to get money to get his family out of eastern Europe, and to show his power. Some things never change
