Carl "Crusher" Creel, the Absorbing Man

 

As he is described in Incredible Hulk #125: "A creature who was once known as "Crusher" Creel public enemy...proven murderer...escaped convict." Creel's position has soften a little over the years (he even went straight for a bit), and as with all Marvel muscle the longer he is around the less serious he's taken. Creel has defeated Thor numerous times, and physically beaten the Hulk. Coupled with Titania, his wife, the two are a force to be reckoned with.
