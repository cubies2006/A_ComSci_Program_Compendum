Attuma

It's easy to think that Attuma the Barbarian might just be another disgruntled Atlantean, but in reality he and his barbarian bretheren are outcasts from Atlantis. According to Namor it is the way things have been since before time was recorded. So it's easy to understand Attuma's quest for conquest of Atlantis. Then one day after Namor had been asked to abdicate his throne a civil war broke out. Attuma settled the civil war, but also implanted himself as ruler of Atlantis.
