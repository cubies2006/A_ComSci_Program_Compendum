The Punisher

Artist: Steve Dillon
Genre: Action/Adventure
Volume: 1

Recommended for Mature Readers

Reviews:
With an upcoming live feature film next year starring Thomas Jane (Deep Blue Sea) and John Travolta (Pulp Fiction, Swordfish), The Punisher will be one of the anticipated films of 2004.

So what better way to get ready for the movie is to introduce a great Punisher book to potential moviegoers (as well as potential new readers).

Frank Castle is a responsible family man (with a loving wife and kid) living a very simple life with a job he really loves and is very dedicated to. Unfortunately, being a very dedicated officer of the law can put your love ones in danger, especially when you get entangled with the mob. So after arresting some members of the mob family, the mob decided to even the points a little bit by gunning down his whole family. Very unfortunately though for the mob they thought they have killed Frank Castle. And you can guess what happened, right? Very violent mayhem ensued!!! Frank Castle now becomes the vigilante known as the Punisher and now dispenses justice his own way.

For people who like non-stop, no-brainer, bullets-flying, michael bay/jerry bruckheimer action movies THE PUNISHER hardcover by Garth Ennis & Steve Dillion (Preacher) is the surest way to get your adrelanine pumping!!!
