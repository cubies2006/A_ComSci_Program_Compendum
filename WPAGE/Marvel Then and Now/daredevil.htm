Origin: Young Matt Murdock, while trying to save the life of an elderly man crossing the street was struck by an oncoming truck that was carrying radio-active materials...He lost his sight becoming Marvel's 1st (& Only?) Blind Super-Hero while obtaining his greatly heightened senses.
