Ultimo

Ultimo was once the bane of many space faring peoples. He was referred to as the Devastator or “He-Who-Destroys”. A group from the planet Rajak that Ultimo had destroyed came seeking vengeance, but Ultimo was too powerful for them. They were forced to flee and led Ultimo into a meteor cluster near the planet Earth. The meteors knocked both Ultimo and those from Rajak to Earth. Ultimo landed in China where he laid inert until he was discovered by the Mandarin.
