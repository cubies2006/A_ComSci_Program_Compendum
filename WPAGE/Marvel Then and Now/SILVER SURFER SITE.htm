SILVER SURFER VOLUME 1 - IN COLOR!
Marvel Comics is publishing a Marvel Masterworks reprint of Silver Surfer Volume 1, the classic Stan Lee/John Buscema stories from the 1960's. The new reprints are in color, unlike the recent Essential Silver Surfer paperback, and in hardback. The books will come in two volumes (the first reprinting the double-sized issues #1-6 and the second concluding with #7-18) and will cost $49.95 in stores, but you can save $15 plus receive free shipping if you preorder from Amazon.com. SAD NEWS - A LEGEND PASSES AWAY
John Buscema passed away on January 10, 2002.  Among his many accomplishments was his art contribution to the Silver Surfer's first volume, most recently updated in The Essential Silver Surfer trade paperback.  Go to the Official John Buscema Web Page for more information about this legendary man and his remarkable contribution to the Silver Surfer and other Marvel Comics characters.
