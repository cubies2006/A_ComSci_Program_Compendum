Name: Bruce Banner

ID: Mostly known

Relatives: Husband of the late Elizabeth "Betty" Ross Banner, Son-in-law of Thunderbolt Ross, Cousin of Jennifer Walters (She-Hulk)...Brian, Father (Deceased), Rebecca, Mother (Deceased).

Affiliates: Founding Member of the Avengers, Defenders & The Pantheon...Ally of Doc Samson & Rick Jones.

Major Enemies: Leader, Tyrannus, Rhino, Absorbing Man, Abomination, Speedfreak.

Powers: Capacity for almost limitless Super-Human Strength, Stamina, & Durability...He also has an extremely high degree of resistance to injury, pain & disease. Can withstand great heat & cold (Anywhere from 3,000° to -190° Fahrenheit). Can leap up to 3 miles in a single bound.

Origin: After being caught in an explosion of a Gamma-Bomb while saving a teenager by the name of Rick Jones, Bruce Banner slowly learned that he has gained unbelievable Super-Human powers, but at a cost!...He would sometimes go on major rampages causing mass destruction & mayhem...His "Jeckyll & Hyde" persona originally was triggered by sunset then by the release of adrenaline from being angered...This has changed many times in his long history.
