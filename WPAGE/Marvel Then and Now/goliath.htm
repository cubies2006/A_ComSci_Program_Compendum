Goliath

Erik Josten, mercenary, attempted murderer, smuggler, scourge of the Avengers, hero? That's right. Goliath's entry on this page is merely wishful thinking on my part. The man who once beat Hercules into a coma is now a hero, a Thunderbolt. The only reason my hope for Erik Josten's return to a life of crime might not be a pipe dream is because Erik views himself as a traitor, as a betrayer. Erik feels he betrayed his sister, causing her death. He betrayed the army by becoming a smuggler and going A.W.O.L. He betrayed, the man who saved his life, Baron Zemo by joining up with the other renegade Thunderbolts. He also feels he betrayed his teammateds helping Zemo escape justice because he felt guilty for turning on Zemo, a man he swore his life to. Josten true to form is keeping secrets from his teammates right now. Will his constant betrayal lead to the Thunderbolts' undoing. If you're a fan of villainy in comics you had better hope so.
