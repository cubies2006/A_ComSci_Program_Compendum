Daredevil Cast and Crew
Marvel has teamed up with 20th Century Fox to bring its legendary comic book hero Daredevil (also known as the Man Without Fear) to the big screen. Attorney Matt Murdock is blind, but his other four senses function with superhuman sharpness. By day, Murdock represents the downtrodden. At night, he is a relentless avenger of justice, haunting the dark streets of New York City, a relentless avenger of justice.
