SHE HULK

Alter Ego: Jennifer Walters Home Town: New York City
Height: 6'1" Weight: 225 lbs
Eyes: Green Hair: Green
H E R O

Group affiliation: Avengers; formerly the Fantastic Four and Heroes for Hire Inc.
Occupation: Adventurer, criminal-defense lawyer
Base of operations: Avengers Mansion, NY City

Caught in the crossfire of a criminal conspiracy, victim of a tainted blood transfusion, lawyer Jennifer Walters found herself transformed into the uninhibited personification of her repressed longing for a life of adventure. The most powerful woman ever to walk the face of the Earth, She-Hulk retains her normal intelligence and personality--but is unable to revert to human form. The emerald attorney has it all; superhuman strength, a courtroom-conditioned mind and gamma-irradiated gams!
