Name: Johann Schmidt

ID: Known

Relatives: Hermann & Martha, father & mother (both deceased)...Sin (Mother Superior), daughter.

Affiliates: Ally to Arnim Zola, Baron Strucker, Crossbones, Taskmaster, Adolf Hitler, Hate-Monger, Baron Zemo I & II, Loki, Kingpin, Mandarin, Dr. Doom, Magneto, Wizard)

Enemies: Captain America, Spider-Man, Magneto, Dr. Doom, Falcon, The Invaders (WW II Team).

Powers: The Red Skull inhabits the body of a clone of Captain America where he is in peak physical condition and also an excellent hand to hand combatant...Gifted marksman & brilliant military & political strategist...Also uses "dust of death" which is a red powder that kills it's victims quickly.

Origin: Johann was originally trained to be Adolf Hitler's right hand man during World War II  after Hitler seen a hatred of mankind in Johann's eyes...The Red skull was used to spread terror throughout the world during and for many years after the war.

The Red Skull made his debut in a Spider-Man comic in a very important tale that told the readers what happened to Peter Parker's parents...Amazing Spider-Man Annual #5 was a story about 2 spies: Richard & Mary Parker who would end up as spies in Algeria during World War II and when the Red Skull found out the true nature of his 2 new friends, they went on a little plane ride never to be seen again.


The Red Skull is to Captain America what The Green Goblin and Venom is to Spider-Man...With many of his appearances in the pages of his arch foe, The Red Skull has met Spider-Man a relatively few times with one of them being in Amazing Spider-Man #366 & 367 in a story that also involved Taskmaster...The Red Skull also played a part in the multi-crossover  Acts of Vengeance  along with a slew of other big-time Marvel villains.
