Name: Norrin Radd (Citizen of Zenn-La)

ID: known

Relatives: Jatarn Radd, Father...Elmar Radd, Mother.

Affiliates: Former Herald to Galactus, Shalla Bal, Mantis, Nova, Warlock, Fantastic Four, Avengers, Defenders, Starfox, Drax.

Major Enemies: Galactus, Mephisto, Dr. Doom, Thanos, and many of the Elders of the Universe.

Powers: Vast Cosmic powers granted by Galactus the planet devourer...Superhuman Strength, ability to channel & expel cosmic energy, can generate beams of energy, skin highly resistant to injury...Use of a surfboard made of unknown materials, psionically linked to the Surfer & can maintain 99% the speed of light.

Origin: To save his dying planet, Zenn-La, from the Planet devouring  powerful being known as Galactus, Norrin Radd volunteered to become Herald to Galactus and aid him in finding new planets to consume and nourish himself...Galactus then used his Cosmic Power to transform Radd into the Silver Surfer and the rest is history!





1st App.: Fantastic Four #48

March 1966

Origin Issue: Silver Surfer V1 #1

Spider-Man Appearances: Silver Surfer V1 #14...Also: V2 #128, Spiderman Team-Up #2, Amazing Spiderman #430 & 431...What-If? V2 #108...Webspinners: Tales Of Spider-Man #4-6



After many appearances in the Fantastic Four, The Silver Surfer receives his first issue of his first of what seems many series!...Issue 14 gives us our first big meeting between The Silver Surfer and Spider-Man...It was a long time till the second one though as became a rare event when the Silver Surfer made an appearance in anyone's title...Eventually the Surfer made a comeback of sorts.

The Surfer hasn't appeared in any Marvel Team-Up's but was featured in the second issue of the short-lived Spider-Man Team-Up and then After Carnage "borrowed" the Surfer's Board for awhile in Amazing Spider-Man #430 & 431, Webspinners featured a 3-part tale set in the past starring the Webhead and the Surfer which was set just after the end of the first Silver Surfer series.
