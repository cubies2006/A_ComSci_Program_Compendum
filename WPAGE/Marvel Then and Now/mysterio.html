Name: I - Quentin Beck (Other Aliases include Dr. Ludwig Rinehart)
II - Daniel Berkhart

ID: Known

Relatives: Elmore Beck, Father...Henrietta Beck, Mother (Deceased)...Maguire Beck, Cousin.

Affiliates: Member of The Sinister Six...Ally of The Wizard, Jack O' Lantern II (Mad Jack)

Enemies: Spider-Man, She-Hulk, Daredevil, Joe Smith

Powers: Skilled Fighter & Athlete, whose major skills are that of an Illusionist & Master Hypnotist. He is also a Skilled Chemist & Stuntman, and a Master Special Effects artist. In past, had ability to emit blasts from costume and even had his cape electrified at one point.

Origin: Originally a Stuntman in Hollywood, Quentin Beck had an idea to simulate Spider-Man's abilities and moved to New York to do so...While there, he worked with the Criminal, The Terrible Tinkerer and costumed himself as an alien...He soon kept up this life of crime after his initial encounter and defeat of Spider-Man.

1st App.: Amazing Spider-Man #13, Also costumed as an alien helping the Terrible Tinkerer in Amazing Spider-Man #2.

Origin Issues: Amazing Spider-Man #13, Chapter One #7

Spider-Man Appearances: Amazing Spider-Man #13, 24 (Not in Costume), 66, 67, 141, 142, 198, 199, 311, 335-339, 407, 408, 413, V2 #8, 12, 17, Annual #1 & 4...Spectacular Spider-Man #51 & 59...Web Of Spider-Man #90...Spider-Man #18-23, 64, 65, V2 #12 & 17...Sensational Spider-Man #0 & 1...Unlimited #4 & 9...Funeral for an Octopus #1-3...Alpha Flight #121...Spider-Man/ Badrock #1B...Webspinners #1-3...Chapter One #7 & 8...Mysterio Manifesto #1-3
