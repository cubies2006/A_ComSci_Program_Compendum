X. Places I Want To Hang Out
  1. SM Shopping Malls

         SM is one the most favorite places that I've hang out with. Because there you can buy almost anything you want, but you must have the money in order to buy them. I also watched the latest movies there. Occassionally, it is the venue of the neophyte foreign artists who would like to promote their song albums as well as to enhance their popularity worldwide. Like for instance, M2M, Steps, and Ant & Dec had a mini-concert at different SM branches like Megamall, City Edsa, City Manila, and others.

  2. Park

         It is one of the most peaceful places that I could go. Because in the park, you can enjoy the cool breeze, the serenity of the place which is very conducive for studying, and the ambiance of the park, the greeness of the place such as trees, grasses, plants, and shrubs. One of the secret of studying is concentration. And in an environment like this it not only refreshes your mind it also soothes your intellectual soul.

  3. National Book Store

         This place is one of the places that I go in SM. Because I always check out the latest books in Star Wars and Calculus Books. I feel very happy whenever my father bought me the books or things that I want whenever we visited National Book Store.

  4. Odyssey

         This is the place, that me and my sister most likely to hang out with. Because we can look up the latest albums of our favorite artists. And in some Odyssey branches, you can listen to your favorite artists' CD tracks which comes handy and you can come back for more some other time.

  5. SM Cinema

         This is one of the place that I want to go with. Because you can enjoy the amplified sound and view the latest movies of your favorite actors and actresses. In addition, it is enjoyable to watch a movie together with your own family. I remember the times when I with my family watched the cinema. One of the most finest memories in cinemas is the first time when I'd watched the first ever cartoon on the big screen. It was me and my father and my sister Athena went to SM City Cinemas to watch "Snow White and The 7 Dwarfs".
